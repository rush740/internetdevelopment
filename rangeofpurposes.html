Range of Purposes

Internet Communication

Internet communication allows you to communicate with people on the web. There are many forms of communication on the internet such as messages, voice/video calls etc. One of the main advantages of these technologies is cost savings.
VoIP technology allows people speak over an internet connection at a much lower cost than traditional telephone networks. Skype and Google Voice are examples.
Email is the exchanging of digital messages over the internet. Its allows people to send documents, links and files quickly. Email is a powerful communication tool for both personal and business purposes. Email providers include Outlook, Gmail and ProtonMail.
Social media platforms allow users to instantly connect, communicate and share information in real time. It also allows companies to advertise and enhance their brands. Channels include Facebook, Instagram and X (twitter).
Instant messaging is a widely used online chat technology that’s facilitates real time communication via internet based chat rooms. These systems allow users to have an uninterrupted communication facility allowing instant collaboration with colleagues and feedback from managers. Yahoo Messenger, Google Messenger and MSN are notable examples.
Video conferencing is a technology that enables groups/individuals to hold face to face meetings. It has become an essential tool for remote working, distance learning. Zoom and Microsoft Teams are widely used.
Online forums are popular message boards that allow individuals to participate in discussions, share information, connect with like-minded people. These virtual communities provide a space for where users can post questions, seek advice and express their opinions on a variety of topics. Boards is a popular online forum.
Online blogs are virtual platforms that enable people to express their thoughts, share experiences, and provide valuable insights on a wide range of topics. Information from personal opinions to expert advice, and in depth analysis can be found in online blogs catering to diverse range of interests. Rough Type and GeekDad are popular blogs.

Internet Research

Internet research refers to seeking information online on topics for a variety reasons. Individuals might seek clarification on a news item maybe looking for a second opinion. People also search for information on health problems to get a better understanding of the symptoms. Students will use the internet when researching for their academic projects and papers. Journalists and writers will search online when researching articles and stories. The internet has also made it possible to interact and connect directly with experts who maintain an online presence, through messaging services, social media channels etc. Internet research provides quick and immediate access to world of information though it can be difficult to sift through and extract meaningful information. The credibility and origin of information and the author can sometimes be difficult to establish.             
Popular search engines include;

  • Google                 Favours fresh content
  • Yippy                    Deep web engine
  • Duck Duck Go    Does not track user information

 

Internet Collaboration

Internet collaboration has revolutionized the way people work together, not being in the same office or even the same country is no longer the barrier it once was. Collaborating online enables individuals from different parts of the world to work together on projects and share ideas.
Real time communication, file sharing, and project management, making it easier than ever to collaborate on complex tasks. It promotes efficiency as team members can work on the simultaneously, providing immediate feedback. Internet collaboration enables companies to avail of the best talent and access to specialized skills and expertise regardless of location. Secure cloud storage allows the sharing and downloading of files anywhere in the world. Some popular collaboration tools are Sync, GitHub, Slack, Zoom, and Monday.

E-Commerce

Electronic commerce has transformed the way people buy and sell goods and services. With the widespread adoption and availability of the internet and the development of digital technologies, ecommerce has grown exponentially. Providing individuals and businesses with the opportunity to engage in online transactions and reach customer across the world. Ecommerce offers convenience and accessibility, virtually eliminating the need to visit physical stores. You can browse, compare and purchase 24/7 from the comfort of your home.      The improvements in logistics, allowing for faster order processing and reliable and timely shipping options. The ability to track and trace packages has enhanced customer satisfaction. Innovations in security in relation to various payment options has ensured can shop and sell safely and securely.
